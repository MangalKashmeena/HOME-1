Who was Walt Disney?

His full name is Walter Elias Disney. Walt was born on December 5th, 1901 in Hermosa, Illinois. His brother Roy help found Walt Disney and he won 22 academy awards during his life. Walt's wife was originally a painter that he hired, Lillian Bounds. Elias Disney, his father was Irish-Canadian. Flora Call Disney, his mother was German-American.From a young age he expressed his love for art, during his childhood he sold many of his paintings in his neighbourhood. In 1911, he moved to Kansas and developed an interest for trains causing him to work a summer job with the railroad selling treats and newspapers around the station and the track. He attended McKinley High School in Chicago until he was sixteen and wanted to join the army for the WW1. Due to being underaged his application was rejected immediately telling him that he wasn't allowed to and he didn't want to give up. Walt still wanted serve his country still, he joined the Red Cross, served in France until 1919 driving an ambulance. Upon returning he wanted to work as a cartoonist so Roy got him a job and soon the brothers started Disney.
